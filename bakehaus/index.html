Baking Tips

無論你是新手還是老手，這些實用技巧都能幫助你的烘焙更上一層樓。

Whether you're a beginner or a pro, these practical tips will level up your baking.

  • 材料回到室溫再操作，口感更細緻
  • Bring ingredients to room temp for better texture
  • 麵粉過篩兩次，蛋糕更加蓬鬆
  • Sift flour twice for fluffier cakes
  • 烤箱一定要充分預熱15分鐘
  • Always preheat your oven for 15 minutes
  • 巧克力隔水加熱，溫度不超過55°C
  • Melt chocolate in a double boiler, max 55°C
  • 使用電子秤精準秤量，告別失敗
  • Use a digital scale for precise measurements
