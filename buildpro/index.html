關於築盛About BuildPro

三十年如一日的堅持Three Decades of Dedication

築盛工程成立於 1995 年，深耕營建產業三十載。我們以嚴謹的工程管理、頂尖的技術團隊，承攬住宅、商業、公共各類工程。Founded in 1995, BuildPro has been a pillar of the construction industry for three decades. With rigorous project management and a top-tier technical team, we handle residential, commercial, and public projects.

每一座建築背後，都是我們對品質、安全與永續的承諾。Behind every structure stands our commitment to quality, safety, and sustainability.
